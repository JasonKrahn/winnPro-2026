#### Project Overview

The 443 Academy Commercial Retail Space represents a sophisticated interior renovation project combining retail, office, and dining functionality. Working with architect Raymond S.C Wan, WinnPro transformed the space with a complete kitchen facility, renovated front entrance, and expanded outdoor patio area. The $845,000 project demonstrates expertise in multi-use commercial spaces requiring careful coordination of food service infrastructure, customer-facing amenities, and back-of-house operations.

#### Results

Completed in June 2018, this project successfully delivered a fully operational commercial space ready for immediate tenant occupancy. The renovation included professional-grade kitchen equipment, refined entrance design, and an inviting outdoor dining area, creating an attractive venue for both retail and food service operations.
